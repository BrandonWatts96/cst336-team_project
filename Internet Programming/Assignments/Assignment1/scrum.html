Developed in 1995, the Scrum method of software development is designed for flexibility and addressing the needs of the team for self-organization.

Advantages:
Since the teams organize the goals in this method, it allows for more in-depth and personalized goals for each team member.

Disadvantages:
Due to the design of the method, the creation of large scale goals is much more difficult in comparison to methods such as the waterfall.
